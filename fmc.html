This page documents most of my solutions and notes for FMC attempts that I have done in WCA competitions. I do not have notes for many of my older solutions, so this history is very much incomplete.

When I started doing FMC, reviewing others solutions was incredibly useful to my improvement, and I hope this is helpful to others too. I'd recommend trying the scrambles yourself first if you did not attend that competition, then review the solution I had. It's very possible that you will do better than me on many of these attempts!

If you find any errors in these solutions or if you find any of my solutions posted elsewhere online that are not included here, please contact me!
